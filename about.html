About Lune Tea

Lune is unique; designed to inspire a commitment to precision in each ex- quisite detail, during every stage of the tea-making process.

Lune is not a shop, nor is it a factory. It is a celebration of the creation, com- plexity and ultimate enjoyment of a tea cup.

We strive for nothing less than excellence.
